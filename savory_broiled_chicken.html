Savory Broiled Chicken

From Metropolitan Cookbook (1964-02-01)

Photo by Martin Widenka on Unsplash

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ready-to-cook broiler-fryer chicken, cut up
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil
  • 3 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 clove garlic, minced

Instructions

  1. Place chicken in shallow baking pan.
  2. Combine remaining ingredients to make a marinade.
  3. Pour over chicken.
  4. Let stand in refrigerator at least 1 hour; turn chicken once.
  5. Remove from marinade.
  6. Place, skin side down, on broiler rack.
  7. Put in preheated broiler 4 to 5 inches from heat.
  8. Broil 12 minutes, basting often with marinade.
  9. Turn; broil 15 minutes or until tender, continuing to baste.
